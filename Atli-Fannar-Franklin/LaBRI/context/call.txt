Dear all,

a post-doc position will be available for one year (and up to one and a half year) in Bordeaux, France, funded by the ANR project "CartesEtPlus" [Maps and More].
https://www.irif.fr/~chapuy/CartesEtPlus.html

The starting date should be between October 2026 and February 2027. There is no teaching duty.

The general topic is in enumerative and algebraic combinatorics, random discrete objects, geometry of surfaces, with an emphasis on maps, trees, and related objects.

The post-doc will work in the Combinatorics group at LaBRI, University of Bordeaux. He or she will be supervised locally and, if desired, jointly by Baptiste Louf (Toulouse) and/or Andrew Elvey Price (Tours). Locally, the project members are Mireille Bousquet-Mélou, Vincent Delecroix and Jean-François Marckert, inside a larger group working in enumerative and algebraic combinatorics.
https://ci.labri.fr/pmwiki.php/Main/HomePage?userlang=en

Applications, in French or English, should be sent before May 15th in pdf format via e-mail to vincent.delecroix@u-bordeaux.fr with subject [Postdoc 2026]. It should contain the following three documents:
- a CV with list of publications and pre-publications,
- a research statement of 5 to 15 pages,
- a short research project, related to the above topics.
Additionally, up to two recommendation letters could be sent separately by their authors to the above address. Please indicate when you would like to start.

Applicants are expected to have a PhD in mathematics, computer science, or theoretical physics when the position starts. Their application should include an expected date for the thesis defense, if applicable.

Decisions will be made by the end of May.
