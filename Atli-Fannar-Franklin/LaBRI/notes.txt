- Breaths of math and CS skills

# Weakness:

- Doesn't generate new ideas. Or rather, hasn't yet set his
  own research agenda. He's good at generating new ideas in the
  context of an existing problem.

# Mixed:

- Atli has quite a personal writing style, which has its charm, but it
  can also be hard to follow.

# Additional context

My association with Atli Fannar is older than the starting date of his PhD:
I was also Atli's advisor on his BSc project. It is known that the
generating function for the Fubini numbers, $F_n$, admits a simple
continued fraction expansion In his BSc project Atli gave the first
combinatorial proof if this result.

As an undergraduate Atli received the Sigurður Helgason award.
